# Privacy Policy for CREDENT

**Last Updated:** February 3, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to CREDENT ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application CREDENT (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We collect the following personal information when you create an account and use our App:

- **Email Address**: Used for account creation, login, and communication
- **Full Name**: Used for identification within the app
- **Employee ID**: Used for staff identification and tracking
- **Role**: Your role as Staff, Representative, or Head of Department (HoD/Advisor)
- **Profile Avatar** (optional): Profile picture if you choose to upload one

### Work-Related Information
- **Work Request Descriptions**: Details of work submitted for CRED points
- **CRED Points Balance**: Your accumulated CRED points
- **Request History**: Records of submitted, approved, or rejected work requests
- **Activity Logs**: History of CRED point credits and debits

### Class and Department Information
- **Class Enrollments**: Classes you've joined (for staff users)
- **Class Information**: Class codes, names, departments (for advisors)
- **Enrollment Status**: Current enrollment in classes

### Device Information
- **Push Notification Token**: Device token used to send push notifications
- **Device Type**: Mobile platform (Android/iOS)

### Usage Data
- **App Activity**: Actions performed within the app
- **Login History**: When you access the app

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

1. **Provide Core Services**
   - Create and manage your account
   - Process work requests and CRED point transactions
   - Manage class enrollments and memberships
   - Track and display CRED points and activity history

2. **Send Notifications**
   - Push notifications about request approvals/rejections
   - Notifications about new work requests (for advisors)
   - Notifications about corrections needed
   - Updates about CRED point changes

3. **Facilitate Communication**
   - Enable advisors to review and respond to requests
   - Allow staff to submit and track work requests
   - Display leaderboards and class statistics

4. **Improve Our Services**
   - Analyze app usage to improve functionality
   - Fix bugs and technical issues
   - Enhance user experience

## Data Storage and Security

### Where Your Data is Stored
- Your data is stored securely on **Supabase** cloud servers
- Push notification tokens are stored to enable notification delivery
- All data transmissions are encrypted using industry-standard protocols

### Security Measures
- Passwords are encrypted using bcrypt hashing
- Secure authentication protocols
- Access controls to protect your data
- Regular security updates and monitoring

### Data Retention
- Your data is retained as long as your account is active
- Upon account deletion, your personal data will be removed within 30 days
- Historical records may be anonymized for statistical purposes

### Supabase
- **Purpose**: Database, authentication, and backend services
- **Data Shared**: All data mentioned above
- **Privacy Policy**: https://supabase.com/privacy

### Expo (React Native)

## Push Notifications

- Push notifications are sent directly to your device
- Notification content is **not stored in our database**
- You can disable notifications in your device settings
- Disabling notifications may limit app functionality

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do **not** sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

### Within the App
- Your name and work requests are visible to your assigned advisor/HoD
- Leaderboard information (name and CRED points) is visible to class members
- Your profile information is visible to users in your classes

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights and safety
- Prevent fraud or security issues
- Respond to government requests

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

### Access Your Data
- View all your personal information in the app's Profile section
- Review your work request history and CRED points

### Update Your Data
- Update your name and profile picture in settings
- Change your password at any time

### Delete Your Data
- Request account deletion by contacting us at manishprakkash@gmail.com
- Upon deletion, we will remove your personal data within 30 days
- Some anonymized data may be retained for records

### Control Notifications
- Enable or disable push notifications in device settings
- Notifications will continue to be sent to your device if enabled

### Opt-Out
- You can stop using the app at any time
- Delete your account to remove your data permanently

## Children's Privacy

CREDENT is intended for use by educational institutions and their staff members. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.
